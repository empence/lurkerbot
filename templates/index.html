LurkerBot for Reddit lurks so you don't have to.

Give LurkerBot a phrase or phrases to watch for, a subreddit to watch, and your email address, and LurkerBot will send you an email alert whenever that word or phrase is mentioned.

Already set up an alert? You can change it or remove it here.
